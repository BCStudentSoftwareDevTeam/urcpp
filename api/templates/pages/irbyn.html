Ethics in Research

Berea College takes its commitment to ethical considerations regarding research involving human subjects very seriously. In 2015, policies were updated regarding how research involving human subjects is reviewed and approved at the College. If your study involves human subjects in any way, it would be wise to review those guidelines now.

All URCPP proposals involving human subjects requre (at least) a draft IRB application at this time. If you have any questions or uncertainty in this regard, it is highly recommended that you discuss your work with the current IRB chair.
